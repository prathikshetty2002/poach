  Step 2: Details 
                 
  Project Details
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                
  ▎ Technical hiring in crypto is broken on both sides. Recruiters cannot tell a real builder from a polished LinkedIn. Builders with strong GitHub histories, hackathon wins, and shipped projects have no portable, verifiable way to signal  
  ▎ this — their reputation lives in screenshots and Twitter threads. Meanwhile, the best Solana devs are never actively job-hunting; they are already busy shipping. The market needs a pull-based discovery primitive, not another job board. 
  ▎                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             
  ▎ Poach.fun is an onchain reputation layer for Solana builders. A builder registers on our portal, connects GitHub, and mints a tradable card whose traits are generated from real signal: commit history, language proficiency, OSS          
  ▎ contributions, hackathon submissions, deployed programs, and project shipments. The card is a living asset — it updates as the builder ships more — and it is transferable, creating the first secondary market for builder reputation.
  ▎                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             
  ▎ On the demand side, companies browse the card index and place USDC-escrowed bids on devs who match their team profile. A poached builder can accept, counter, or ignore. Layered on top is an AI recruiter agent that ingests a JD, filters 
  ▎ the card index by role requirements (Rust seniority, Anchor experience, DeFi domain, activity recency), and returns a shortlist with evidence-linked rationales — turning a week of manual sourcing into a 30-second query.
  ▎                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             
  ▎ The agentic workflow is core, not cosmetic: card trait generation, skill extraction from OSS code, and JD↔builder matching are all AI pipelines. Every step is reproducible, audit-logged, and grounded in on-chain and GitHub evidence so  
  ▎ recruiters can verify claims before they bid.
